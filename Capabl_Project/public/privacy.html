VytalCare Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

VytalCare uses Google Sign-In to authenticate users. Depending on the features you use, the application may store health-related information such as medication reminders, activity data, and profile information.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized health tracking.
  • Store medication reminders.
  • Synchronize selected health information.
  • Improve application functionality.

Data Sharing

VytalCare does not sell or rent your personal information. Data is used only to provide the application's functionality.

Medical Disclaimer

VytalCare is intended to assist users with health tracking and reminders. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the VytalCare development team.
